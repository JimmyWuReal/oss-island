# Code of Conduct

Oss Island contributors are expected to make participation welcoming and safe.

Be respectful, assume good intent while addressing impact, give actionable feedback, and do not harass or discriminate. Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, or other contributions that violate these expectations, and may temporarily or permanently ban repeat offenders.

Report conduct concerns privately to the project owner through the security contact method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md). Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible.

This project follows the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
